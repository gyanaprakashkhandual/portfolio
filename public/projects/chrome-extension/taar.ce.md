# Taar

Speak any language. Write with precision. Taar lives inside every text field in your browser — speak, translate, and polish your words in any language before you send.

## Overview

Taar is a free Chrome extension that embeds directly into every text input field in your browser, giving you a voice-first multilingual communication assistant wherever you type. Users can speak naturally in any language, translate their words across 130+ languages with context-aware accuracy, and use AI to refine their message tone and style — all without leaving the page they are on. With over 47,000 active users and more than 2.1 million messages translated, Taar has become an essential tool for global teams, professionals, and anyone who communicates across language barriers daily.

## Key Features

- Voice-first input that captures speech and converts it to polished text instantly
- Translation across 130+ languages with context-aware accuracy
- AI Polish to refine messages — formal, friendly, concise, or persuasive
- Works inside every input field across Gmail, Slack, LinkedIn, forms, and documents
- Custom AI prompts for precise rewrites such as bullet lists or tone adjustments
- Privacy-first architecture — text is never stored or sold
- Completely free with no account or signup required

## What Makes It Stand Out

Taar is not a standalone app — it lives directly inside every text field in the browser, making it frictionless to use. Unlike traditional translation tools that require tab-switching or copy-pasting, Taar activates in place with a single mic click. The combination of voice input, real-time translation, and customizable AI polish in one zero-setup extension makes it uniquely powerful for global professionals who communicate in multiple languages daily.

## How It Works

**Step 1 — Install the Extension:** Add Taar to Chrome in one click, completely free.

**Step 2 — Speak or Type:** Hit the mic icon anywhere and speak in any language, or type your message directly.

**Step 3 — Polish and Translate:** Taar instantly refines your words and translates to your target language.

**Step 4 — Send with Confidence:** Paste directly or hit send — polished, clear communication every time.
