# Mega Jewelers

A complete e-commerce platform designed for jewelry businesses with product management, inventory tracking, secure user access, and integrated sales management.

## Overview

Mega Jewelers is a full stack e-commerce platform built specifically for jewelry businesses to manage their digital storefront, inventory, and sales operations. The platform enables administrators to manage products, track inventory levels, process customer orders, and monitor business performance through a centralized dashboard. Customers can browse jewelry collections, view product details, and place orders through a clean and intuitive interface. The system ensures secure authentication, efficient product management, and scalable architecture for growing businesses.

## Key Features

- Complete e-commerce platform for jewelry products
- Advanced product catalog management
- Inventory tracking and stock management system
- Secure authentication and role-based access control
- Integrated order and sales management
- Admin dashboard with analytics and reports
- Product image upload and media management
- Responsive UI for seamless user shopping experience
