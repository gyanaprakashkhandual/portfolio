# Feel

A web application focused on emotional well-being and mood tracking, helping users reflect on and understand their daily feelings.

## Overview

Feel is a web application designed to support emotional awareness and mental well-being by allowing users to log, track, and reflect on their daily moods and feelings. The platform provides a safe and simple space for users to record their emotional states and view trends over time, promoting self-awareness and mindfulness.

## Key Features

- Daily mood and emotion logging
- Mood history and trend visualization
- Reflective journaling support
- Clean and calming user interface
- Persistent mood data storage

## Features

- Daily Mood Logging
- Mood History and Trends
- Reflective Journaling
- Calming and Intuitive UI
- Persistent Emotion Data Storage

## What Makes It Stand Out

Feel stands out by combining mood tracking with reflective journaling in a calming interface designed to reduce friction for emotional self-expression, making it accessible and encouraging for daily use.
